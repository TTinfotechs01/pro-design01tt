Market & Application

INDUSTRIAL COATING

Industrial coating
  • Automotive
  • Consumer electronics
  • Flooring & Furniture
  • Industrial metal
  • Mobile & Cosmetic case
share
Automotive
Our specialized UV-curable Oligomers and Monomers can address the demanding requirements of automotive coating. In addition, support from our global technical experts is always available to help you solve any challenges you face today.
  • Headlamp Coating (Reflector and Lens)
  • Interior Plastic
Consumer electronics
Our high-performance products are suitable for matt and smooth plastic substrates by maintaining hot water and scratch resistance. Please try out our guide formulations that are recommendable for especially spray applications.
  • Excellent Hot Water Resistance
  • Excellent Matting Property
  • Excellent Abrasion Resistance
Flooring & Furniture
Demand for UV resin and water-borne products is growing more than ever, with a clear shift from the existing solvent-borne systems.

To suit the trends, our UV curable polyurethane acrylates, polyester acrylates, and epoxy acrylates can meet the needs of your high standards to protect your flooring and furniture.
  • Bio Products
  • BPA Free Oligomers
  • Water Based UV Products (UVPUD, Water Soluble)
Industrial metal
We have the technology to develop specialty products with high reactivity and excellent adhesion on industrial metal.

Optimizing the composition of our resin and the guide formulation can assist you in overcoming fundamental challenges and improving the performance of the industrial metal coating.
  • Excellent Corrosion Resistance
  • Excellent Weatherability
  • Excellent Adhesion
Mobile & Cosmetic case
For the mobile and cosmetic case coating application, optimizing high scratch resistance while maintaining chemical resistance and flexibility is the heart of the performance.

Test our products in your formulation to achieve an optimal balance of flexibility and rigidity.
  • High Scratch Resistance
  • High Temperature/High Humidity Resistance
  • Excellent Chemical Resistance
  • Good Balance Flexibility and Toughness
